About me

Hi, I'm Mohamed Amine Chekrouni! I am a full-stack web developer and a mathematics teacher working at the Ministry of Education in Morocco. With over five years of experience in both web development and teaching mathematics, I bring a unique combination of technical expertise and teaching skills to my work.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science, which has given me a solid foundation in both areas. My education has equipped me with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in my current role as a full-stack web developer and mathematics teacher.

I started my career as a front-end developer, working at a company for 5 months. After that, I transitioned to a role as a mathematics teacher at the Ministry of Education, where I have been teaching for 4 years. However, I never stopped my passion for web development and started working as a freelance web developer in my free time.

As a web developer, I have a proven track record of delivering high-quality web solutions that meet the needs of my clients. My front-end development skills include HTML, CSS (using Scss and Tailwind to generate CSS), and JavaScript using jQuery as a framework. On the back-end, I utilize PHP and MySQL, and I use AJAX to send requests to APIs.

In addition to my web development skills, I have experience designing UI/UX for my projects. I use Figma to design UIs, Illustrator to design logos and icons, and Photoshop to edit images. My experience in UI/UX design allows me to create web solutions that not only function well, but also look great and provide an enjoyable user experience.

As a mathematics teacher, I bring a deep understanding of mathematical concepts and a strong ability to communicate complex ideas in a simple and accessible way. I am dedicated to helping my students succeed, and I am always looking for new and creative ways to engage my students and help them build a lifelong love of learning.

In addition to my technical skills and teaching experience, I am a self-starter who is always eager to learn and grow. I believe in continuous learning and improvement, and I am always exploring new technologies and techniques to stay up-to-date in the fast-paced world of web development.

When I'm not coding or teaching, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, and I am always looking for new challenges and experiences. I believe in the power of technology to make a positive impact on people's lives, and I am excited to be a part of this dynamic and ever-evolving field.
